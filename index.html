Portfolio

LYFE - Hackatrix 2018

LYFE is an app that allows you to generate online service tickets for both customers and "non-customers" of a bank. When the ticket is generated, the customer is located with the nearest agencies or, failing that, he can choose the district where he will be attended.

Tools: user interviews, benchmarking, user flows, customer journey, prototype of low and high fidelity, figma and marvel.

CLUB AVENTURA

Club Aventura is a customer loyalty system for Mall Aventura, it was made through Service Design. The customer gets points for being in the mall or buying and redeeming their purchase tickets.

Tools: user interviews, benchmarking, user flows, customer journey, prototype of low and high fidelity, figma and marvel.

TalentGo - Everis

It is a virtual platform in which the applicant will have access to a profile where they will be able to test their technical skills, and the recruiter will have access to them, being able to corroborate them; this way you can evaluate them in an efficient way, saving time and money. At the same time, the recruiter will have access to a profile in which they can constantly check the stages of their requirements processes, access data from past candidates and be able to better filter the needs in terms of the profile sought.

Tools: Moments, archetype, diagram design, service design, figma and marvel.

Financiatech

Financiatech is an online financial advisor that allows you to invest the savings of young professionals between 18 and 30 years. This financial adviser guides the investment according to the profile that each person has and takes as objectives the motivations, as well as the goals of each one.

Tools: Testing Tools, affinity map, user personas, user research,paper prototyping, figma and marvel.

Here Maps

It is a mobile application that allows users to track and monitor routes that take place on outdoor / outdoor trails, aimed at lovers of adventure activities (mountains, lakes, parks, etc.) in Peru. This app allows you to search nearby routes, record them and share photos, videos in real time. In addition to access offline maps and share your location in real time.

Tools: User research, testing tools, prototyping interaction, maps, figma and marvel.

Viaggio

Viaggio is a website that allows you to make comparisons between the main services provided by interprovincial buses, and also shows a map with the route and stops. All this with the aim of providing comfort and safety to passengers who choose a bus.

Tools: Storytelling,affinity map,user personas, adobe illustrator, figma and marvel.

Warmi trip

Warmi trip is a social network oriented only for traveling women. In this you can post photos as well as plan them with a budget and suggestions. In addition to this we offer an emergency button in case you find yourself in a dangerous situation and need help.

Tools: HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, user research,Bootstrap 3 y marvel.

Foodmap

FoodMap is an application that allows you to find restaurants near your location according to the area, among them we have typical food, fish and seafood, etc. Additional to this shows the location, capacity, payment methods of the restaurant, if it is petfriendly and its website.

Tools: HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, user research,Figma y marvel.
